         THE LEVEL-HEADED CHOICE

             Meet Level Head: Greene King's very own unfiltered, perfectly balanced
             session IPA. This easy-drinking pale ale brings a smooth sip with a hoppy
             aroma that balances tropical fruitiness with crisp grapefruit notes. Light,
             refreshing, and expertly crafted – it’s the pint that keeps things steady no
             matter what the day throws at you.

             Pair it with classic pub grub for an unbeatable match. Fancy a burger? The
             citrusy hops cut through the richness like a dream. Tucking into a mouth-
             watering pie? The crisp, clean finish is a perfect counter to the rich
             fillings. And if you’re after a snack? Spicy nachos or salty fries are made
             even better with Level Head’s fresh, sessionable style.

             Centuries in the making, poured in the moment – Level Head is the kind
             of pint that’s always a good call.
